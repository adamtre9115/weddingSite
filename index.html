Welcome to the AdamLoveAffair!

Tresean's Perspective

So, Ashley and I knew of each other long before we began dating. Our story begins back in 2012 when she was a contestant in the Miss Kappa Kappa Psi Pageant at North Carolina Central University. I happened to be her escort that year but, the funny thing is that we rarely ever spoke. However, I always thought that she was beautiful. The only thing was I didn't want to be that guy who tried to talk to the girls simply because they were in our pageant. Moving forward, the times when Ashley did speak to me were when she was stalking me for the money she won after earning the title of Miss 1919 in the pageant.

Fast-forward to New Years Eve of 2013, I had company over but I still felt completely alone. That night before going to sleep I asked in prayer for God to place a woman in my life that he knew that I could be happy with. In January of 2014, Ashley and I happened to be at a meeting together. I could tell that she was completely frustrated so, afterwards I decided to check to see if she was okay. From that moment forward, we spoke to each other nonstop. That led to me finally working up the courage to ask her on our first date. She said yes of course (although I found out that her best friend forced her to go out with me, I will always be glad that she said yes).

I asked Ashley to be my girlfriend for the first-time March 7 (she claims that she said yes then but, I didn't hear it so, I asked again several times before I finally heard yes). From then on, I have tried to make sure that I am the man that she will always be happy and in love with because I know that I have found an amazing woman.

Ashley's Perspective

So, our journey went a little like this… My sophomore year of college I ran for the title of Miss Kappa Kappa Psi. Tresean happened to be my escort for the pageant. Throughout the entire pageant process, I can’t even remember him and I having one conversation or even speaking to one another. Due to raising the most money out of all the contestants I earned a monetary gift and of course, with Tresean being the treasurer I had to communicate with him to get my coins. He claims that I stalked him for the gift, but I only asked him once we met up and I never used his number again honestly.

While serving my reign as Miss 1919 I attended a meeting that didn’t end well and I left heated!!! I got to my room later that night and I received a text message from Tresean asking if I was “okay” of course I responded and we found ourselves talking to one another every day since then. After late-night phone conversations, Tresean finally worked up the courage to ask me on our first date. I’m sure you can guess that it went well. Those long conversations turned into seeing one another every day until he finally asked me to be his girlfriend. Let Tresean tell it and he’ll claim that he asked me several times but, I only remember him asking once. Nevertheless, I said yes and our journey began from there.

Falling in love was the easy part; planning a wedding - yikes!
