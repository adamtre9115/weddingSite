Welcome to the AdamLoveAffair!

Tresean's Perspective

So, Ashley and I actually knew of each other long before we began dating. Our story actually begins back in 2012 when she was a contestant in the Miss Kappa Kappa Psi Pageant at North Carolina Central University. I actually happened to be her escort that year. The funny thing is that we rarely ever spoke but, I thought that she was absolutely beautiful. The only thing was I didn't want to be that guy who tried to talk to the girls simply because they were in our pageant. So, the times when Ashley did speak to me were when she was stalking me for the money she won after earning the title of Miss 1919.

Fast-forward to New Years Eve of 2013, I had company over but I still felt completely alone. That night before going to sleep I asked in prayer for God to place a woman in my life that he knew that I could be happy with. January of 2014, after all of this time, Ashley and I finally began talking to each other after a meeting that we both attended. From that moment forward, we kept talking to each other and that led to me working up the courage to ask her on our first date. Although I found out that her bestfriend forced her to go out with me, I will always be glad that she said yes.

I asked Ashley to be my girlfriend for the first time March 7 (she claims that she said yes then but, I didn't hear a yes so, I asked again several times before I finally got a yes). From then on, I have been trying to make sure that I am the man that she will always be happy and in love with.

Ashley's Perspective

I don’t have enough space on the website to begin to explain our journey, but I’ll attempt to sum it all up. Early January 2014, I was at a point in my life where I had a lot of issues going on and battling mild depression due to the loss of my best friend. Going on with my life and completing my reign as Miss 1919 for the Zeta Sigma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi National Honorary Band Fraternity Inc. I began to get frustrated (KKPsi will make you turn up, but you got to love them lol). Tresean was my escort for the KKPsi pageant; however, we never talked I would just secretly admire him from afar. However, after leaving a pageant meeting extremely frustrated I received a message notification from Tresean and we haven't stopped talking since.

On March 8, 2014 Tresean asked me to be his girlfriend after spending almost every day together for several months of course I said yes! Though he claims he asked me a couple of times to be his girlfriend, which I don’t recall (Tresean has a way of making up stories). As our relationship progressed and time went on we faced many challenges, but we always somehow got through them. I knew Tresean was the one for me after just a year of dating, my family accepted him as their own son and all I could think of this is the ONE, he’s my blessing that I’ve been praying for. Meeting a man that was smart, driven, and dealt with my attitude was a keeper. After both of us completed graduate school we decided to move in together, which is was something life changing for me, being that I’ve never lived on my own before. Shortly after moving to a new city together and starting my career, our relationship changed dramatically. Although we were still boyfriend and girlfriend things felt so much different and, on November 12, 2016 giving the man of my dreams a simple YES!!!! was the game changer. This is just the beginning of what God has planned for US!

Falling in love was the easy part; planning a wedding - yikes!
